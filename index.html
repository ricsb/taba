Chase the Truth !!


This is a first idea about how could be a "Personal Web-Based Periscope Simulator", which could be used to train submariners in specific skills.

The "intelligent" tutor will guide you by throwing some alert messages (he is doing his best, please be cool!).

If you are capable to stand without jeopardizing your crew and submarine, until one target pass by you, a big secret of humanity will be unveiled!!

Once every model are loaded, you can see a little tutorial, if you will, hitting "3" on your keypad. Anytime you need to remember the key controls, just hit "1".
